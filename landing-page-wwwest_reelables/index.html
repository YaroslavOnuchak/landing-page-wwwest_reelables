Reelables’ labels come on reels and feed into industrial printers. They stick to packages, shipping labels, boxes of inventory, safety equipment, and even tools & equipment.

How it works

Provisioning takes place at the point of manufacture or distribution when inventory is ordinarily barcoded. Reelables are activated with an external NFC scan and associated using existing serialisation processes. This process can include custom printing on industrial printers modified with our activation hardware.

Reelables emit industry standard Bluetooth beacons. These signals are received in the background by a broad range of listening gateways, including employee phones.

As inventory moves from location to location, the Bluetooth signals are automatically received. This generates events such as geo-fence crossings, change of custody, or even temperature fluctuations.

These events trigger customisable actions defined by cloud-based workflows that maintain state for each label. With minimal coding, our Reelogis API logic layer provides a smart, low-bandwidth way to implement business processes as labeled inventory moves through your supply chain.
